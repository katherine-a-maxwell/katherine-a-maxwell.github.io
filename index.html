About Me

I am currently a project researcher (postdoc) at the Kavli Institute for the Mathematics and Physics of the Universe (Kavli IPMU), located near Tokyo.

Before that I was a postdoc at the Max-Planck Institute for Mathematics (MPIM) in Bonn, Germany.

I graduated from my PhD at the University of Minnesota in 2021.

I majored in physics and mathematics at Truman State University, Missouri’s only public liberal arts and sciences university.

Originally I am from Carbondale, Illinois USA, which is part of Little Egypt and the upland south.
